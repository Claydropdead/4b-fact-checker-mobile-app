What is 4B Fact Checker?

4B Fact Checker is a professional AI-powered mobile application designed to address the growing challenge of misinformation and disinformation in the Philippines. Using advanced artificial intelligence, the system cross-references digital content with trusted Philippine sources and verified databases to provide data-driven verdicts—whether information is true, misleading, or false. This platform empowers citizens to make evidence-based decisions and strengthens our collective digital literacy.
